TAKE A CLASS

Sarah Glass Ceramics is a woman-owned pottery studio based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. We offer pottery classes for beginners and experienced artists alike, providing a supportive and creative environment to explore the art of pottery making. Our studio space offers equipment and expert guidance to help you channel your inner artist.

Returns Policy

No refunds or returns are available for classes or workshops offered by Sarah Glass Ceramics.
